What is body language?

Body language is a type of a nonverbal communication in which physical behaviors, as opposed to words, are used to express or convey the information.
Such behavior includes: facial expressions, body posture, gestures, eye movement, touch and the use of space. Although body language is an important part of communication, most of it happens without conscious awareness.

Getting ready for a job interview?

It is very important to practice your body language as much as you rehearse your answers to common interview questions. Natural tendencies tend to take over when you are nervous, so you have to learn to consciously overcome them. You can practice with friends or an interview coach to ensure you are fully aware and in control of your body when the time comes for the interview.
